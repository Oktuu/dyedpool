Based upon Marvel Comics Wade Wilson is a former spetsnaz soldier working as a mercenary in New York . In the local bar of his friend Ferdinand Wade meets a prostitute Vanessa Carlisle, with whom he later has a romantic relationship. A year later, Wade makes a proposal to Vanessa, and she agrees. Suddenly Wade faints and the next day he is diagnosed with cancer in the last stage. Vanessa asks Wade to stay with her, but Wade does not want her to see his death.
